Capstone Project Part I

The Capstone Project is a final year project for Computer Engineering Technology course, this project is divided in two parts and it is work upon the aglie methologoy. There are in total 5 sprints to be completed by the duration of 8 months. First two sprints were completed in 4 months and second other three sprints completed in next 4 months. In the first two parts of sprints we focus on developing a RC car which runs on Micro Python MCU and controlled by a wireless controller that uses an RF Transmitter and Receiver. In addition, we have also install an Webcam which will server live feed using rasberry pi.

Capstone Project Part II

In the second part, which is Sprints 3/4/5 we were focusing on developing on robotic arm and the wireless controller. Moreover the testing this of robotic arms were done on two different arms first assembling them and then calibrating them. After that, In addition we have Built a design of robotic arm which was mounted on the front of the car and it runs on Raspberry Pi 3 Model B and to control the robotic arm we have use and Logitech Wireless Controller F710 which controls the servo motors of the arm.
